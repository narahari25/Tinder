Our website helps college students find their perfect roommate match quickly and easily. To start, users create a detailed roommate profile that includes information on their lifestyle preferences, living habits, and housing preferences. Users can then browse other profiles and swipe right on those they're interested in, or swipe left if they're not a good fit. If two users swipe right on each other, they are matched and can start chatting to see if they would be a good fit as roommates.
